PRODUCTE

LinguaLive fosters student's practical application of language skills acquired in the classrom by providing a plaft
for learners to engage in structured conversations with native speakers. We collaborated with secondary languag
students and professional linguists on this project after observing that language learners need a way practice
speaking because the goal of learning a new language is to have real-world conversations.



THE SCOPE

  • Create web responsive platform for students to practice speaking the language the are learn with native speakers.
  • Enable students to engage in conversation topics of personal interest.
  • Give students a sense of progress through structured dialogues that leverage knowledge acquired in the classrom.
  • Allow native speakers to provide constructive feedback to foster student's development.

The problem

After interviewing several language students, we came up with a problem statement than encompasses the key pain
point we aimed to solve with design thinking.




Language learnes need a way to practice speaking because the goal of learning a new language is to be ablt to have real-world conversations.








Cross platform experience

In order to make the on-demand learning experience more accessible, we deemed web responsivity to be crucial to our
users. This allow students to easily have conversations at any time or on the go.
